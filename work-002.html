002 : vernon buam

vernon buam is an artistic pseudonym made from parts of my actual name - vernon being my middle name taken from my late grandfather and buam being a shortened anagram of my last name. under this moniker I pursue and exercise the ultimate source of pleasure in life - discipline. the audio pieces are a fusion of all of the musical skills i've picked up in life beginning with my debut album "abyss" but following that as i began taking interest in vinyl record sampling I'd notably begin using a technique I refer to as "microsampling" where i was cutting and sampling such small bits of audio and making it as different from the original as i could while retaining some of the original flavor. discipline ep was how i got my chops cutting samples but the later albums knxwing vol.1 & soul sublimation are good examples of the microsampling technique fused with instrumentalism
