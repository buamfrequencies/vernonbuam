001 : 木漏れ日 | komorebi

komorebi is a Japanese word that describes the effect of sunlight streaming through the leaves of trees. when you traverse a forest and you are hit with warm, leafy, branch-filtered sunbeams - that's komorebi.

“Any patch of sunlight in a wood will show you something about the sun which you could never get from reading books on astronomy.

These pure and spontaneous pleasures are patches of Godlight in the woods of our experience.”

C.S. Lewis
