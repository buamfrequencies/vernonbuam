003 : mega-all

mega-all is an instrumental musical project that takes it's namesake from a powerful materia of the same name in a classic JRPG for the Playstation 1 known as Final Fantasy VII. The project is aptly named because it encompasses everything buam had learned musically up the the point of this projects inception and the goal was to try everything he hadn't before. Having lost many of his instruments and years of work on dead musical relationships with people who only wanted to be proximal to fame, buam began soul searching, returning to his roots and rebuilding his repertoire of instruments and spent time creating music in styles he had intentionally avoided previously. A gentle blend of multi-instrumental improvisation and experimentation would lead to some of his favorite works. These works are so precious to him they've yet to be officially released, awaiting more patient mixing and mastering. The raw version of the first album is available on soundcloud.
